## Zhipeng Gao

Embedded systems and firmware engineer with 20+ years of experience spanning server management controllers, industrial automation, and AI accelerator platforms.

### What I Do

I specialize in the intersection of hardware and firmware — designing and building the low-level software that makes servers and AI infrastructure work reliably. My focus areas include:

- **Server BMC firmware architecture** — full-stack design from Redfish APIs down to device drivers
- **AI accelerator integration** — bringing GPUs and AI accelerators (NVIDIA, AMD, Intel) into server platforms with unified out-of-band management
- **Industry standards** — active member of DMTF and NVMe organizations, driving adoption of PLDM, SPDM, and Redfish in AI server scenarios
- **Hardware validation tooling** — building tools for device bring-up, debugging, and performance evaluation
